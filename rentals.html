Our Shop offers many services for your biking needs
One of these services is rentals. Rentals are a great thing because they offer an inexpensive way to bike without fully commiting to a purchase

Road Bikes

Road Bikes are a great new addition to our rental services. These bikes are great for going long distances very quickly. We offer two different Road Bikes to help you achieve that new year goal.

This is our standard road bike package, It comes with a helmet, lights, and a newly tuned up bike. We charge $20 per day or a $100 per week for this package.

This is our performance package for our Road Bikes. This package comes with a helmet, lights and a tuned up carbonfiber framed bike. We charge $30 a day or $150 a week for this package.

Mountain Bikes

Road Bikes are great but they're not exactly the best for an off road adventure. To supplement this, we two have time tested mountain bikes to help you realize your more adventurous side.

Our standard Mountain bike package is a great deal for anyone that wants to get into trail riding. This package includes a helmet and flashlight on a standard mountain bike. The fee is $25 a day or $120 a week

Our trail bike package is for experienced mountain bikers that know they're way around a Mountain Trail. We offer a helmet, flashlight attachment, and waterbottle holder on our trail package. The fee is $35 a day or $175 a week

All Rental times must be disclosed at time of rental purchase. If bike is not returned by 8 PM of last rental day an additional $50 fee will be added for every day after the end. Calls can be made to extend rental time. Damaged bikes will have an addition fee for repair.
